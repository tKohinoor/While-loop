🛡 Code of Conduct

Our goal is to provide a welcoming, respectful space for everyone in the While(True) community.

🔹 Be respectful

No harassment, hate speech, trolling, or discrimination of any kind. Be kind, be cool.

🔹 Collaborate, don’t compete

This isn’t Stack Overflow. We learn and grow together — no gatekeeping allowed.

🔹 Keep it clean

Don’t spam, self-promote excessively, or post NSFW content. Stay on-topic and helpful.

🔹 Report issues

If you see something concerning, let us know via Discord or email mods@whiletrue.dev

Violations may lead to warnings, muting, or removal from the community.
